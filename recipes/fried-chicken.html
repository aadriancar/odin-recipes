Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

This crispy fried chicken recipe begins by making the breading mixture with flour, paprika, salt, and pepper, which is placed in a zip-top bag for easy shaking and mixing. The chicken is then dredged in buttermilk, ensuring itâ€™s fully coated, before being transferred to the flour mixture. The chicken is shaken well in the bag to coat it evenly and then placed on a baking sheet to allow the coating to set into a paste-like texture.

Next, the chicken is fried in hot oil in a skillet. First, the chicken is browned on both sides, then the heat is reduced, and the skillet is covered to cook the chicken through for about 30 minutes. After removing the cover, the heat is increased again to make the chicken extra crispy. Finally, the fried chicken is drained on paper towels to remove excess oil, resulting in a golden, crispy exterior with a juicy interior.

Ingredients

  • Four-pound whole chicken OR four pounds of any type of chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
